## Editor



>[!TIP]- To fix ordered list not updating in live preview or editing mode
>Press "Shift+Tab"


>[!TIP]- To bring back last closed page
>Press "Ctrl+Shift+T"


>[!TIP]- To find accidentally deleted file
>Go to .trash folder and sort by date accessed
>
